Description of Shua Qalaqshi.

"In the middle of the city" is a Georgian sitcom genre series about a group of friends who live in one of Tbilisi's districts (Vakeshi). The series is produced by Studio Night Show. The series was aired on Imedi TV for six years. The first season premiered on September 23, 2007, and the last, sixteenth season ended on July 9, 2013. On March 3, 2011, the feature film "Once in the Middle City" was released. Midtown was updated on October 13, 2014 as "10 Years Later". Its show ended in March 2017.

Format

The series is filmed in the format of a sitcom. The Night Show Studio borrowed this format, as in the case of other programs ("The Night Show", "The Saturday Show", "The Candidate" and others) from American analogues (eg "Friends").In the summer of 2007, the staff of the Night Show Studio went to America, met with the producers and screenwriters of popular sitcoms, attended the recording process. In addition, he trained at Warner Brothers Studios and earned the right to do this format. The show's style and humor became the reason for its mass popularity. The first series of the series aired on September 23, 2007. The series was aired every Saturday and Sunday at 10 pm on Hope channel.

Story and characters

The series tells about 8 friends Irakli, Marika, Dato, Anano, Sandro, Eka, Ketevani and Tamari, who live in the plains of Tbilisi. Irakli Chkheidze is a doctor and works in the main hospital of the country. Marika is Irakli's wife and a housewife, Dato Aleksidze is Marika's cousin and an artist, who, after divorcing his wife, moves into an apartment with his cousin Marika and his wife Irakli. Eka is a manager of a successful company and is a classmate of her friends Marika, Anano, Ketevani, Tamari and Sandro, Anano is an agro-specialist and a classmate of her friends, she loves nature and animals very much. Sandro Makharadze is a lawyer who received his education in London and is very proud of it, although in reality he is not as educated as he describes himself. Ketevan and Tamar Ghlonti are sisters who live next door to Irakli and Marika and are classmates of friends.The series is divided into 2 stages: for all 10 seasons, the same characters participated in it, and from the eleventh season, a new stage began and most of the old characters of the series were replaced by new ones.

The end of the series

At the end of July 2013, the sixteenth season of "Midtown" and with it the series as a whole ended. The air time of "Shua Qalaqshi" on TV-Imedi was occupied by a new situational comedy - "Our Story".

The return of the series

In October 2014, it became known that "Shua Qalaqshi" would return to TV viewers with the old composition and a new name - "Shua Qalaqshi 10 years later"[1]. The renewed series aired on "Tv11" and "Maestro". After the end of the first season, the TV company "Imedi" returned to "Shua Kashti" from the second season. The series was closed after the seventh season. The final, tenth episode of the latter aired on March 25, 2017.
